## Product Summary (Marketing-Relevant Only)

Peak Fettle is a fitness app. The core differentiator is **cohort-matched competitive percentile rankings** — users see where they rank vs. people of the same age, gender, experience level, and weight class. No other major app combines this with adaptive AI plans.

**Free tier hook:** Percentiles are free. This is the viral/acquisition feature.
**Paid tier hook:** AI-generated personalized plans that adapt over time.
**Target users:** Gym-goers with any level of seriousness — the competitive/social feature bridges casual and serious users.

---

## Value Equation (Governs All Marketing)

> **Value = (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood of Achievement) ÷ (Time Delay × Effort & Sacrifice)**

Every piece of copy, every screenshot, every CTA must maximize the numerator (dream outcome = knowing your rank + improving it) and minimize the denominator (fast onboarding, immediate percentile reveal, no friction).

---

## The One Defensible Content Angle

**"Where do you actually rank?"**

Every piece of content must make users feel the desire to know their percentile. Zero generic fitness tips. Zero form breakdowns. Zero transformation before/afters (saturated). The only defensible content niche is the competitive ranking hook — because Peak Fettle owns it and no major creator does.

Examples that work:
- "You're stronger than 87% of gym-goers your age — here's how we calculated it"
- "What's your actual gym percentile? Most people are wrong about where they stand"
- "I tracked my bench press for 6 months and here's what the data shows vs. 10,000 other lifters"

## Launch Sequence

**Month 0–1:** Warm outreach → first 200–500 users → first 50 app store reviews. ASO keyword research. Lean content 3x/week. Paid ad micro-test $50/week for conversion rate data only.

**Month 2–3:** Micro-influencer outreach (10–20 contacts, powerlifting niche). Content velocity up. Referral mechanics built but not yet pushed.

**Month 3–4:** Percentile pool large enough for share cards. Launch challenge invites. Activate referral campaign.

**Month 4–6:** Scale paid ads on proven organic hooks. Apple Search Ads on competitor keywords. ASO iteration.

**Month 6+:** Paid amplification of winning creative. Community formalization (Discord, subreddit).

## Unit Economics (Marketing-Relevant)

- **Max CAC to hit 3:1 LTV:CAC:** ~$25–33 (assuming ~$80–100 LTV per paid subscriber at $9.99/month × ~10-month retention)
- **Typical fitness app CPI via paid:** $1.50–$4.00 — there is margin if free-to-paid conversion rate is sufficient
- **Apple Search Ads conversion rate:** 50–70% on brand keywords, 15–25% on competitor keywords
- **Content marketing 3-year ROI:** 844% average — but requires unique angle and 3–6 months before results

## ASO Priority Keywords

Primary: "workout tracker," "gym progress tracker," "strength percentile," "personalized workout plan"

Screenshot strategy: lead with social proof framing — "See where you rank vs. 10,000 lifters." This is the differentiator. Solicit reviews at peak engagement moments (post-PR set, post-first percentile reveal).

---

## Micro-Influencer Profile

Target: powerlifting meet competitors, strength coaches, natural bodybuilding prep coaches. Audiences pre-disposed to tracking and competition.

Offer: free lifetime Pro + 20% revenue share on attributed installs (unique promo codes). Content brief: "Show your real percentile rank" — authentic product use, no scripted promo language.

Start with 10–20 influencers in the 20K–80K follower range. Treat as a portfolio experiment, not a single bet.
