## Settled points

- **Frequency is higher than powerlifting at every tier** because skill is the limiter — beginners 3 days, intermediate 4–5, advanced 6+ (with two-a-days).
- Block periodization is the standard for intermediate+ — accumulation → intensification → realization/peak.
- Long post-meet deloading periods (3+ weeks, ~2–3 sessions/week, ~half session length, different exercise selection) happen twice yearly after the two main meets. This is distinct from in-cycle back-off weeks.
- Larger/older/more advanced athletes need longer, deeper tapers.

## Known-issue watchlist

- Equipment-substitution honesty is critical for app users. Resist the temptation to imply DB/KB versions are "equivalent" — they train transferable power, not the sport skill.
- Watch for new biomechanics literature on bar-path optimization and the second-pull / catch transition; revise drill recommendations if it shifts.
