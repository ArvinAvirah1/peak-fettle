## Settled points

- The dominant message: **going from nothing to something** matters more than program perfection. Twice-weekly training of all major muscle groups is the headline ACSM message.
- Beginner can grow on 8–12 hard sets/muscle/week; intermediate 10–20; advanced 15–25+ with cycling.
- Each muscle ideally trained ≥2×/week at every tier above untrained.
- Progression mechanism shifts: session-to-session (LP) → weekly (double progression / WUP) → mesocycle (block / DUP).
- Deload cadence shortens with tier: rarely needed at novice → every 4–6 weeks intermediate → every 3–4 weeks advanced.
