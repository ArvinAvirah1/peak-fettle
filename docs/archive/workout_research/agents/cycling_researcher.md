## Settled points

- ~80/20 intensity distribution holds. Pyramidal in base/build is time-efficient and effective for raising FTP; polarized takes over near racing.
- Avoid sweet-spot during pure base — straight Z2 gives the same aerobic benefit with less fatigue. Reserve sweet-spot for build.
- Cycling tolerates substantially higher weekly volume than running due to low impact; advanced riders 10–15+ hrs/week is normal.
- Strength training (2–3×/wk, heavy, ≥80% 1RM, 4–8 reps) improves MAP, threshold power, sprint power, and efficiency — without harming endurance.
- On-bike big-gear low-cadence intervals are a valid substitute for off-bike squats when equipment is unavailable.

## Known-issue watchlist

- Indoor-training platforms (Zwift, TrainerRoad, Rouvy) evolve fast — keep the "smart trainer" descriptions current.
- Watch for new evidence on durability training and over-unders.
- Power-meter pricing and ubiquity has shifted what "available equipment" means for the average user; revisit assumptions yearly.
