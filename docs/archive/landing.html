What Peak Fettle does

One app. Every discipline. Real numbers.

A slide through everything Peak Fettle gives you — built around the way real training actually unfolds.

Progress Tracking

Tracking that knows the difference between a deadlift and a 5K. Lifters get sets, reps, weight, auto-calculated 1RM estimates, and a normalized strength score (Wilks/DOTS or our Peak Fettle score that rewards volume, progressive overload, and consistency). Cardio gets splits, pace trends, and consistency. Manual logging at launch — wearable sync (Apple Watch, Garmin) on the roadmap.

Competitive Percentiles

Opt in and see exactly where you stand. Percentiles are matched to your age, gender (with a unisex toggle), and years in the sport — so a beginner is never stacked against a 10-year veteran. Honest at the top, encouraging at the bottom.

Personalized Plans

Paid users take an opening survey — goals, current level, injuries, equipment, time available, what you actually like doing — and the AI builds a plan grounded in seasoned-coach recommendations. Swap exercises, adjust volume, reschedule days. Enjoyable plans are consistent plans, and the AI adapts as you log. Body composition goals are guided toward sustainable, attainable targets — you always have the final say.

Habit & Streaks

Showing up is the rep. A five-minute gym visit counts, because the routine itself rewires the habit loop. Miss a session? You have a same-week make-up window. Real emergencies — exams, illness, travel — get a manual override. Streaks only break if two sessions slip with no make-up and no override. No shame, just consistency.

Free Tier

Full progress tracking and competitive percentiles in the free tier — already ahead of most competitors. Includes a library of static templates (Push/Pull/Legs, Upper/Lower) you can follow as-is. The paid tier unlocks the fully personalized, adaptive AI program.

Consistency is the only fitness metric that compounds. Everything we built starts there.
