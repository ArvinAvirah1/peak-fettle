## Settled points (preserve across refreshes unless directly contradicted)

- Beginner LP delivers excellent results for the first 6+ months — do not replace with intermediate templates prematurely.
- Intermediate progression mechanism is undulating periodization (WUP/DUP) with scheduled deloads every 4–6 weeks.
- Advanced lifters benefit from block periodization (Accumulation → Transmutation/Intensification → Realization/Peak) with explicit competition tapers.
- RPE anchors: RPE 10 = max; RPE 9 = 1 rep left; RPE 8 = 2 left. RPE-8 triple ≈ 85–87% 1RM.
- Equipment ladders for the barbell movements are cleanly substitutable; flag when DB/bodyweight substitutes shift the movement pattern (e.g., conventional deadlift → DB RDL changes which muscles dominate).

## Known-issue watchlist

- Volume-landmark literature is still in flux; re-check Renaissance Periodization and Stronger By Science yearly for MEV/MRV revisions.
- Watch for new meta-analyses on training-to-failure proximity and its interaction with intermediate/advanced progression.
